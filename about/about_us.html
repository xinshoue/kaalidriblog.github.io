At Kaalidri we value the privacy and trust of our users above everything else. We do not store any personal data or logs that could be used to identify you. Our focus is on building a secure, reliable, and user-friendly platform where you can feel safe and confident.

We are continuously working on new features, including the option to create accounts in the future. When this becomes available, we will ensure that your privacy remains fully protected, with no unnecessary personal information required.

If you come across any issues or have suggestions, please contact us through the contact form so we can address them as quickly as possible.

Thank you for supporting our work — we’re excited to keep improving and hope you enjoy your experience with Kaalidri.
